Beehive Check at Finley Farms

Where: Finley Farms

When: Saturday, September 4 at 10 AM

Register: https://finleyfarmsmo.com/happ...

"Learn all about bees with Resident Farmer and Beekeeper Liesel. The class starts on The Workshop patio where you’ll get suited up in a protective beekeeping jacket and veil. You’ll learn about the parts of the hive, how to handle frames, and gain insight on bee stages of development and their life cycles. During the workshop, you’ll also look at eggs and larvae, find queens, see how bees store nectar and honey and see Farmer Liesel identify and address any hive problems in real-time. Attendees are encouraged to wear light-colored pants and closed-toed shoes. No beekeeping experience is required. In the event of rain, hive checks will not be rescheduled. Attendees will be refunded."

Ozark Trail Hike + Piney River Brewery

Where: 37 North Expeditions - Meet at Farmers Park

When: Saturday, July 31st from 1-3 PM

Register online at 37 North Expeditions

"Nothing screams summer more than a swim session in an off-the-beaten-path Shut-In! We will explore the breathtaking Ozark Trail through the Klepzig Mill to Rocky Falls section. This is a 6-mile hike with a 459' elevation gain. The hike will begin at Klepzig Mill, a historic mill located in the beautiful Rocky Creek shut-ins. We will then continue through Buzzard Mountain shut-ins before ending at Rocky Falls, which is one of the most remarkable cascades in Missouri. You will not be disappointed by its beauty! Throughout the hike, we will take several breaks to immerse ourselves in the stunning Ozark's waterfalls, old historic buildings, watering holes, and dynamic terrain. Make sure to bring your bathing suits as we will be jumping into the numerous shut-ins located along this hike. On the drive back, we will stop at Piney River Brewing Company for some great locally made beer and handmade pizza. The brewery is very family-friendly"
