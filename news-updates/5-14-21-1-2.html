Friday Five: 417's Best Events 6/11-6/17

Share

So Much Fun So Little Time

You know what to do! Check out this week's fun activities in the 417!

What's Happening This Week?

Free Swim Day at Ozark Municipal Pool

Where: Neal Grubaugh Pool - Ozark, MO

When: Saturday, June

Cost: Free with Non-Perishable Item

"Join us at the Neal and Betty Grubaugh Municipal Pool for the annual Free Swim Day! But remember to bring a non-perishable item: 1 Non-Perishable Item = 1 Free Entry; Items will be donated to Ozarks Food Harvest"

7th Annual MO Food Truck Fest

Where: Chance of Sno & Coffee Co. - 308 W Mt. Vernon St, Nixa, MO

When: Saturday, June 12th

"Let’s CELEBRATE! Come hang with us for fun, games, giveaways, prizes for the kiddos, and actual SNOW! We’re busting out the snow machines to bring fluffy stuff down from the sky! We’ll of course have our amazing handcrafted coffee drinks and our famous Taiwanese shaved sno desserts! If you haven’t tried it yet, now’s the time!! We are so excited to be open in our new bigger + better trailer serving our full menu - rumor has it we’ll also be introducing a couple of NEW menu items at this event, too!"


Movies at Founders Park

Where: Founders Park - Springfield

When: Fridays & Saturdays @ 9 PM

Cost: Free

"Come see Movies at Founders Park. Enjoy new release and classic movies in an outdoor setting. Bring your own lawn chairs and blankets. Movies are FREE and begin at dusk. More info at ParkBoard.org/Movies"

Members Only at The Riff

Where: The Workshop at Finley Farms

When: Sunday, May 30th @ 2 PM

Cost: $15

"Come Rock & Dance with Members Only at the coolest music venue in Springfield. All of your favorite 80s hits in 3 hours! Tickets are $15. Purchase your tickets/tables online or at the door. Show starts at 9pm. Doors open at 8!
Cover photo: Chet Smith Photography"

Click here for tickets

SEArenity Yoga

Where: Wonders of Wildlife

When: Thursday, June 17th 7:30-8:30

Cost $15

"Join us in our open ocean exhibit for a unique yoga experience. Our certified yoga instructor will lead you through hours of relaxation and restorative yoga practices to suit both newcomers and seasoned yogis alike! Please bring a yoga mat, and any props you may need (blocks, blanket, bands, etc). We can’t wait to SEA you!"
