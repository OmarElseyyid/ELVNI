Friday Five: 417's Best Events 7/23-7/29

Share

Never Too Hot To Make Memories

Another hot action-packed weekend is coming your way! Ever wish time would slow down and you had more time to enjoy the weekends? Make the most of the time you do get! Check out this week's blog post to indulge in all of the best events!

What's Happening This Week?

Ozark Booster Club Rodeo

Where: 1101 N Riverside Rd, Ozark, MO 65721

When: Friday, 23 - Saturday, 24 at 8 PM

Come enjoy a local rodeo!

Night Swim @ Ozark Municipal Pool

Where: Neal Grubaugh Pool

When: Friday, July 23rd from 6:30-9:30 PM

Cost: Free

"Location: 907 E Parkview, Ozark, MO

Ages 2 and Under: FREE

Ages 3 and Up: $3"

Finley Farms Career Fair

Where: The Workshop at Finley Farms

When: Saturday, 10 AM - 5 PM

"Apply and interview for full- and part-time opportunities including food & beverage positions, groundskeeping, security and more.This full-day hiring event features on-the-spot job offers, live music, refreshments and tours of The Farm and The Ozark Mill.See all of our openings at https://finleyfarmsmo.com/jobs/ "

Moonlight Madness

Where: Silver Dollar City

When: Saturday, July 24th from 9:30 AM-10 PM

"Enjoy over 12 hours of nonstop fun for 16 straight days during Moonlight Madness at Silver Dollar City! We're open from 9:30am to 10pm, so ride all day and into the night with extended ride time on over 40 rides & attractions.And, BONUS: 2 Midnight Saturdays* extending playtime to the max!"

Saturday Date Night Wheel Throwing

Where: Creava Studio

When: Saturday, July 24th from 5 PM - 7 PM

Cost: $60 dollars per couple

"Are you looking for a fun & unique date night experience? Have you ever wanted to try throwing a pot on the wheel or just want to play in the mud with the one you love... come enjoy a 2 hour adventure. You will learn the basics of wheel throwing and then you will each get a piece fired & glazed to save as a keepsake of your date.We regularly have Date Night Wheel Throwing so be sure you select the date that works best for you. Classes can accommodate up to 2 couples. If you want your date night to be private you will need to call the studio to set aside a night.Date Night Wheel throwing cost $60 per couple"
