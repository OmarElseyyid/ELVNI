Friday Five: 417's Best Events 7/16-7/22

Share

A Whole Lot of Fun

This weekend is packed with things for people of all ages! I know I for one wish I could go to every single thing! Before booking your weekend make sure to check out what's going on in the 417 land!

What's Happening This Week?

Friday Night Farm Tour

Where: The Workshop at Finley Farms (starts on the workshop patio)

When: Friday, July 16th @ 4 or 6 PM

Cost: $15

Tickets: https://finleyfarmsmo.com/regi...

"Resident farmers Brendan and Liesel show you around the greenhouse, mushroom yard, high tunnels and beyond while providing insight on the organic standards and practices employed by Finley Farms.

In addition, you’ll receive a complimentary drink to take on your tour. Please note, all farm tours start on The Workshop patio."

Designed Sisterhood Night - James River South & Joplins Campus (& Online!)

Where: James River South & Joplins Campus (& Online!)

When: Friday, July 16th 7-10 PM (Events start at 5,

Cost: Free

"Designed Sisterhood Night with our dear friend, Lisa Harper!

Hosted at James River Church - in person at two campus locations - Ozark and Joplin, MO and Online

Watch on Facebook, YouTube, and designedforlife.org

It's a FREE EVENT - for girls of all ages! (10-100) with food trucks, photo booths, boutiques & more!"

Mom's Night Out: Coffee Q + A

Where: Black Lab Coffee Co

When: Friday, July 16th from 6:30-8:30

Cost: Free

"MOPS/MomsNext Republic will be hosting a COFFEE FLIGHT NIGHT at Black Lab Coffee Company! Come try some flights with us, available for $8.99! We will have members of our Leadership Team in attendance to answer questions about our MOPS & MomsNext group (open to moms of babies in utero all the way through a Senior in High School!). We will also spend time getting to know each other and/or staying in touch through the summer! Find Your People, join us at MOPS/MomsNext Republic!!"

Finley River Movie Nights - Minions

Where: Ozark Finley River Park

When: Saturday, July 17th @ 8 PM

Cost Free

"Join us for your new family tradition, Finley River Movie Nights! All movies are held at the Finley River Park at the north end of the park on the ball field, begin at dusk, and are FREE! Please bring lawn chairs or blankets. Concessions will be available for purchase. All movies are dependent on weather but will be rescheduled to a later date. No smoking and no alcohol."
